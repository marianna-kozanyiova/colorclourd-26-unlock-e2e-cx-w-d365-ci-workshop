# ColorCloud 2026, Hamburg: Unlock end-to-end customer experience with D365 Customer Insights

[Last updated: 2026-04-10]

[Reading time: 1 min]

A hands-on workshop for building an end-to-end customer journey with Dynamics 365 Customer Insights - Data and Journeys through a fictional ColorCloud scenario.

## Fictional ColorCloud Scenario
ColorCloud is a fictional direct-to-consumer brand that sells connected lifestyle products. Over the past few years, the company has grown quickly and expanded across multiple touchpoints, including its website, mobile app, and engagement channels. As a result, customer data is spread across multiple systems, teams, and touchpoints. To address this, ColorCloud has adopted Dynamics 365 Customer Insights - Data and Dynamics 365 Customer Insights - Journeys to create a more connected and personalized cross-channel customer experience.

During today's workshop, you will configure an end-to-end customer journey for Maya Novak, a first-time buyer who discovers the brand online, in your own Dynamics 365 Customer Insights - Data and Customer Insights - Journeys environments. Maya signs up for a discount, buys ColorCloud Aura, a smart ambient light controlled through the ColorCloud mobile app, receives onboarding communication and an accessory recommendation, activates the product in the app, and later shares feedback.

Moving forward, we will refer to Customer Insights - Journeys as **CI-J** and to Customer Insights - Data as **CI-D**.
