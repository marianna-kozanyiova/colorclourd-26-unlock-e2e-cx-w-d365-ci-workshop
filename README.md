# ColorCloud 2026, Hamburg: Unlock end-to-end customer experience with D365 Customer Insights
A hands-on workshop for building end-to-end customer journey with Dynamics 365 Customer Insights – Data & Journeys through a fictional ColorCloud scenario.

## Fictional ColorCloud Scenario
ColorCloud is a fictional direct-to-consumer brand that sells connected lifestyle products. Over the past few years, the company has grown rapidly and expanded across multiple customer touchpoints, including its website, mobile app, and customer engagement channels. As a result, customer data is now spread across multiple systems, teams, and touchpoints. To address this, ColorCloud has decided to adopt Dynamics 365 Customer Insights - Data and Dynamics 365 Customer Insights - Journeys to create a more connected and personalized cross-channel customer experience.

During today's workshop you will configure an end to end customer journey for Maya Novak, a first-time buyer who discovers the brand online, within your own Dynamics 365 Customer Insights - Data (CI-D) and - Journeys (CI-J) environments. Maya signs up for a discount, buys the product: ColorCloud Aura, a smart ambient light controlled through the ColorCloud mobile app; gets an onboarding packet and relevant accessory recommendation, activates it in the app and later shares feedback.
